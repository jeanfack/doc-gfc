He wants to optimize his notification settings to ensure he reviews the most urgent notifications first, especially during busy workdays.\

*Choose THREE correct answers.*
:::

:::{grid-item-card}
* {bdg-success}`Correct` Pull requests where his review is requested.
* {bdg-success}`Correct` CI workflow failures for all repositories he is a member of.
